About Me

I’m a computational linguist currently working at the Chair of Comparative Philology at Julius-Maximilian University of Würzburg. I’m developing a digital tool for the study of valency classes in historical languages, with a focus on Classical Armenian.

My main research interests lie in speech processing, machine translation, and quality evaluation. I enjoy combining programming with linguistics and am especially passionate about building tools that bridge the gap between language and technology.
